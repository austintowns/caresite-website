## 6. How It Works

**Step 1 -- Connect**
Link your Google Business Profile and PointClickCare instance. Caresite imports your locations and discharge data automatically -- no manual CSV uploads or IT projects required.

Set your outreach cadence and Caresite handles the rest. Families receive a brief, respectful SMS after discharge. Positive sentiment routes to your Google listing. Negative sentiment routes to a private form your team reviews internally.

Watch your review volume climb from one every 10 months to 10 or more per month. Use the analytics dashboard to track ratings, respond to reviews with AI-generated replies, and dispute policy-violating content automatically.

## 10. FAQ

**Q1: Is Caresite HIPAA compliant?**
Caresite is HIPAA-safe by architecture. Our platform never stores, processes, or transmits protected health information. Discharge timing data is used only to trigger outreach -- no clinical data, diagnoses, or patient identifiers flow through our system. Because no PHI is handled, no Business Associate Agreement is required.

**Q2: How does sentiment gating work?**
When a family member clicks the review link in their SMS, they first answer a brief satisfaction question. If their sentiment is positive, they are directed to your Google Business Profile to leave a public review. If their sentiment is negative, they are routed to a private feedback form that your team can review and respond to internally. This protects your public rating while ensuring every voice is heard.

**Q3: What is the AI dispute assistant?**
The AI dispute assistant automatically scans your incoming negative reviews against Google's review policies. It identifies potential violations such as fake reviews, conflict-of-interest posts, reviews containing PHI posted by the reviewer, and off-topic content. It then generates a pre-written dispute submission your team can file directly with Google.

**Q4: How does the PointClickCare integration work?**
Caresite connects to your PointClickCare instance via a secure API to receive discharge event notifications. When a resident is discharged, Caresite automatically queues an SMS to their designated family contact at your chosen delay interval. No clinical data is imported -- only the discharge event, contact name, and phone number.

**Q5: How quickly will we see results?**
Most facilities see a measurable increase in review volume within the first 30 days. On average, our customers move from approximately one review every 10 months to 10 or more reviews per month. Star rating improvements typically follow within 60 to 90 days as new positive reviews dilute older negative ones.

**Q6: Can I use Caresite across multiple facilities?**
Yes. The Standard plan supports up to 10 locations on a single dashboard, and the Enterprise plan supports unlimited locations. Each facility maintains its own campaign settings, analytics, and Google Business Profile connection. Regional and corporate users can view aggregate reporting across all locations.

**Q7: Will AI-generated review responses expose us to HIPAA risk?**
No. Caresite's AI response engine is specifically trained to never confirm or deny a patient or resident relationship. Every generated response acknowledges the reviewer's feedback in general terms and invites offline follow-up. Our responses have been reviewed by healthcare compliance attorneys and are designed to meet CMS and OCR guidance on public communications.

**Q8: How is Caresite different from Podium, Birdeye, or Reputation.com?**
Those platforms serve restaurants, dentists, auto dealers, and dozens of other industries. Caresite is built exclusively for skilled nursing facilities. That means HIPAA-safe architecture from day one, PointClickCare integration, F-tag and survey correlation, SNF-specific AI response training, and a dispute assistant that understands healthcare review patterns. We do not offer a generic product adapted for healthcare -- we built the product around your compliance and operational reality.
