Generic review platforms treat nursing facilities like restaurants. Caresite was architected from day one for the regulatory complexity, survey cycles, and operational realities of skilled nursing. PointClickCare integration, F-tag awareness, and survey process workflows come standard -- not as an expensive add-on you'll never get configured.

### Pillar 2: HIPAA-Safe by Architecture
Other platforms rely on staff training and hope. Caresite strips PHI client-side before data ever leaves the facility, generates AI review responses that cannot reference protected information, and maintains a complete audit trail. Your compliance officer will actually approve this one.

### Pillar 3: Turn Negative Reviews into Operational Intelligence
The sentiment-gated flow routes positive experiences to Google and negative feedback to your internal team -- before it becomes a public problem. The AI dispute assistant identifies Google policy violations in existing negative reviews, giving you a documented path to removal instead of hoping they age off.

## 4. Objection Handling

### "We don't have time to manage another platform."
Caresite runs on autopilot. Discharge-triggered review requests go out automatically via PointClickCare integration. AI drafts every response. Your team approves with one tap -- 5 minutes a week, not 5 hours.

### "We already have a review management tool."
Is it flagging F-tag-relevant feedback before your next survey? Is it stripping PHI before a staff member accidentally mentions a resident's condition in a response? Generic tools create compliance risk in SNF environments. Caresite eliminates it.

### "Our reviews are fine -- we have a 3.8 star rating."
Your competitors in the market are at 3.2. That sounds comfortable until you realize most SNFs get one new review every 10 months. A single 1-star review sits at the top of your profile for nearly a year. One bad discharge experience is 12 months of reputational damage.

### "We can't justify the budget right now."
At $149/month, Caresite costs less than losing a single private-pay admission to a competitor with better reviews. The average private-pay SNF resident generates $8,000-$12,000/month in revenue. How many admissions are you losing to a 3-star Google profile?

### "HIPAA concerns -- we can't send patient data to a third party."
You're right to be cautious, and that's exactly why Caresite exists. PHI is stripped client-side, meaning protected information never leaves your network. No other review platform in the market can make that claim. We'll walk your compliance team through the architecture.

### "We tried something like this before and staff wouldn't use it."
Generic platforms fail in SNFs because they require staff to change workflows. Caresite plugs into PointClickCare -- the system your team already lives in. No new logins, no new apps, no training days. Review requests trigger from events already happening in your EHR.

### The Compounding Cost of Inaction
Every month without active review management is a month where your oldest 1-star review defines your facility to every family searching Google. Unlike restaurants that get buried under new reviews quickly, SNFs have so few reviews that a single negative one dominates your profile for 6-12 months.

### Your Competitors Are Starting to Move
The SNF industry has historically ignored online reputation. That window is closing. Early adopters are building review velocity now, and the facilities that establish a 4.5+ rating first will own local search for years. Reputation compounds -- the facility that starts today has an 18-month head start over the one that starts next year.

### Survey Season Is Coming
CMS survey results are public. Families cross-reference Google reviews with survey scores. A strong Google profile softens the blow of a deficiency, and a weak one amplifies it. Getting review velocity up before your next survey window is the difference between controlling the narrative and reacting to it.
